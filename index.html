MY LIBRARY
HOW TO ADD A BOOK
  • You have to click the "Add Book" button and a form will be displayed with the data you have to complete.
HOW TO EDIT A BOOK
  • When you click the "Edit Books" button, the edition mode will be activated and a red border will be displayed around books.
  • You can only edit the read status or delete a book.
  • To delete a book, click the "x" sign in the top-left corner on the book you want to delete.
  • To change the read status, click the read section on the book.
  • To escape edition mode, you have to click "Done" button.
